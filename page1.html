Mango School 

We are looking for donations to Lescota Foundation to help to complete the Mango School. Mango project is the hope for many children in Matekela, Mbinga, Tanzania. This is a pre and primary English medium school. The school is under construction and support is needed to complete unfinished clasrooms.
