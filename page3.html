About Us

Elimu for Children is a project which operates under Lescota Foundation (registration number 00NGO-0005594).

MISSION

To provide access to education to children. Our main focus area is Tanzania with the goal to expand globally. 

VISION

To help children gain quality education and foster the creation of economic opportunities. 

OBJECTIVES

To help the development of schools in Tanzania/the rest of the world;
To provide sponsorship for children's education;
To create a high quality online education platform and reach areas which lack in teachers, textbooks and study materials.
